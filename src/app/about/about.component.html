Welcome to Give Life!

We're here to spread
kindness and help those who need it
in Sri Lanka.


Our Goal

At Give Life, we want to make things better for people in Sri Lanka who don't have much.
We believe that when we work together, we can make a big difference, even with small acts of kindness.

What We Do

On our website, you can find different ways to help out.
We show you projects that help kids get food,
help families in need, and support education for those who can't afford it.

Being Open and Clear

We think it's really important to be honest about what happens with the help you give.
That's why we promise to always show you how your kindness is making things better.
When you give through Give Life, you'll see the good you're doing.

Join Us

Whether you're just one person
who cares or a company that wants to do good, Give Life is a place for you.
Together, we can bring smiles and change lives.
